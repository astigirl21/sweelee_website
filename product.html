Amplifier ($1,499)

The PRS Archon is a commanding 2-channel amp with versatile overdriven tones and sparkling clean with plenty of headroom. Designed with five gain stages before the master volume, the Archon’s lead channel is voiced to cover everything from Classic Rock to Metal with full, lush distortion. The clean channel provides rich tones that retain clarity even at high volume, and there is ample headroom, creating an excellent platform for pedals. The Archon has a remarkably responsive tone with incredible note separation, whether you’re playing on the clean channel or chugging on the lead.
